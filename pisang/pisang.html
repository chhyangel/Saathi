Pisang is a village development committee in Manang District, Nepal, Situated in the Marshyangdi River valley, Pisang lies south of Pisang Peak, north of Annapurna II, and west of Paungda Danda.
beauty of Pisang.
Pisang is one of those places that show the best of Nepal: scenery, mountains, culture, and generosity of spirit. It is in these smaller posts where hospitality is most welcoming, families are kind, and guests have the opportunity to learn what life is really like in the Himalayas.
